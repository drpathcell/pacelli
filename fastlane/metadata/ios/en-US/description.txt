Pacelli is a private, encrypted household management app for families and housemates.

Manage tasks, checklists, plans, inventory, and shared notes — all in one place, with your household.

PRIVACY FIRST
Everything sensitive is encrypted end-to-end on your device before it ever leaves. Your household data belongs to you.

FEATURES
• Tasks & subtasks with due dates, priorities, and categories
• Checklists for shopping, packing, routines
• Plans with flexible entries and checklist steps
• Household inventory with barcode scanning, expiry tracking, and low-stock alerts
• House Manual — a shared knowledge base for your home
• Calendar view with upcoming tasks and expiring inventory
• AI Chat assistant built in — ask about your household data
• Works offline with local storage option

HOUSEHOLD SHARING
Invite members to your household. Keys are shared securely — only your household can read your data.

DATA PORTABILITY
Export your entire household as an encrypted backup. Import it on any device.
