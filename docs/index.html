Welcome to Pacelli

Pacelli is a household management app designed to help families organize tasks, checklists, plans, calendar events, inventory, and a shared house manual — all in one secure, easy-to-use app.

📋 Tasks & Checklists

Organize household tasks and checklists with due dates, subtasks, and categories.

🗓️ Calendar

Plan family events, appointments, and important dates in a shared calendar.

📦 Inventory

Track household supplies, groceries, and items with expiry dates and low-stock alerts.

📖 House Manual

Create a shared knowledge base with household procedures, tips, and important information.

🔐 End-to-End Encrypted

Your household data is encrypted before leaving your device — even we can't read it.

👥 Family Sharing

Invite family members to your household and stay synchronized across all devices.

Privacy & Security First

Your household data is yours. We don't sell your information, run ads, or track you. All user-generated content is encrypted end-to-end, and you can delete all your data at any time using the "Burn All Data" feature.
